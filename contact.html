Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project take?

It depends on the scope. A simple logo design might take 3–5 days. A full brand identity or 3D scene can take 2–4 weeks. I will always give you a clear timeline before we start.

How do revisions work?

Every project includes a set number of revision rounds. I aim to get things right without unnecessary back-and-forth.

What file formats do you deliver?

For design: PDF, PNG, SVG, AI. For 3D: PNG renders, FBX or OBJ models on request. For music: WAV and MP3.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I work with clients from anywhere in the world. Communication is done online and files are delivered digitally.

How do I pay?

Typically a deposit is required to start, with the balance due on delivery. I accept bank transfer and PayPal.

Can you work to a specific deadline?

Yes, as long as the deadline is realistic for the scope of work. Let me know your deadline upfront and I will confirm before we begin.
